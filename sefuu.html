1st Place (Classic Track) Dubhacks 2018

Introduction

I attended my first hackthon in October 2018 at the University of Washington in Seattle. I was in charge of the design and also worked on the front-end using HTML, CSS, and React.js. My team consisted of Amir, John, Harry, and Jatin. We decided to make a safe travels web app using Node, Mongodb, Google Cloud Services, Azure, Firebase, and Redux.

The Problem

Students often feel unsafe commuting home alone during the wee hours of the day. In Seattle, although the crime rates have dropped drastically since 1980, the number of incidents involving robbery, aggravated assault, and rape is already 3000+ in just 2018 alone.

The Solution

Sefuu aims to give students a piece of mind by allowing them to find the safest way home, not just based on the fastest route possible, but also based on the number of crimes in the area.

Logo

Sefuu means "safe" in Japanese. We chose our logo with a destination pin and heart because it represents safely arriving at the destination.

Login Page

Easily sign in using Google or Facebook. This enables users to connect with other familiar users.

Map

The crime rates are displayed using a heat map. The crime type (robbery, assault, gun-warning) is displayed with icons.

Route

Once your destination is chosen, our map will take you through the safest route by avoiding areas with crime.

Twitter Integration

A live twitter feed that shows crime incidents reported by the Seattle police.

Travel Companions

Because you can sign in using social media, you can find mutual friends who are also planning to take the same route at that time. The app has a chat service that allows you to connect with a possible traveling companion which will make you less of a target to predators.

Exta Feature (not shown)

To make it more accessible for everyone, we have different colour modes to cater to those with visual impairements. We also have a dark mode to allow you to use the app safely at night without drawing more attention to yourself.

Future Considerations

If we were to continue with this project in the future, we will expand this app to parse crime rate statistics from al over the world to provide the safest route for you wherever you are.
